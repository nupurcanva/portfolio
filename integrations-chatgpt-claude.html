üéØ Context & Problem Definition

The creative landscape underwent a seismic shift in 2024-2025. ChatGPT rose from 1% to 5% of user searches. This represented a fundamental transformation in how people approached creative work.

The Core Problem

Four Critical UX Gaps Identified:

  1. Conversational Expectation Gap: Users expected back-and-forth iteration like traditional LLMs, but CanvaGPT only provided one-off design outputs
  2. Context Switching Friction: Users had to manually discover and install CanvaGPT, breaking their natural workflow
  3. Limited Design Control: Users couldn't customize designs within the AI interface, forcing premature handoffs to Canva
  4. Brand Consistency Challenges: No ability to apply brand guidelines or maintain visual consistency across AI-generated designs

üîç Discovery & Research Process

Comprehensive User Journey Mapping workshop

We identified eight distinct stages in the current journey, each with pain points: discovery friction, manual plugin search, missing brand/context, generic generation, limited preview, iteration misinterpretation, loss of history on handoff, and manual publish steps.

Diary Study Methodology

  • Week 1: Baseline behavior observation using current CanvaGPT
  • Mid-point intervention: concept testing of enhanced integration capabilities
  • Week 2: Post-concept usage tracking and iteration feedback

Design Strategy & Framework

Product Design Principles: Seamless context-aware transitions, optimize for speed to value, scalable design system, progressive disclosure, meet core needs & user intent, parity with Canva AI, show the value of Canva, build lasting relationships.

Defining the AI-Human Handoff Framework across design complexity, user control preferences, and technical feasibility.
